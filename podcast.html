COMING SOON

From the birth of video games to Nickelodeon nostalgia,
this podcast highlight the people and places that shine
a light—or THE light—in our lives. I hope you enjoy this
nostalgic show, which features interviews and random facts
we absolutely don’t need but have fully convinced ourselves
we do. All obsessive personalities who use television and
Mario Party as a relief and defense mechanism are welcome.

Love & awkward silence xx
